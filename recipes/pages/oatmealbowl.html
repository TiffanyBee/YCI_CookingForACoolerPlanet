Oatmeal Bowl

  • 4 Servings
  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 5 minutes
  • Inspired by twopeasandtheirpod.com

Especially if you’re an athlete, having a bowl of oatmeal in the morning will leave you full of energy. The use of the microwave cut down on wasted energy, due to being both small and efficient. Once fully cooked, top it off with your favorite berries, fruits, and nuts!

INGREDIENTS

    • Toppings

DIRECTIONS

  1. 1Fill a bowl with the old-fashioned rolled oats and water.
  2. 2Let the bowl fill for 5 minutes.
  3. 3Put the bowl in the microwave for 2 minutes.
  4. 3Add your desired toppings!

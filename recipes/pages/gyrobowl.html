Gyro Bowl

  • 4 Servings
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Inspired by reciperunner.com

The variety of produce featured in this recipe could be bought from a local farmers market or the “ugly” produce section of the grocery store. This coupled with the poultry section of this bowl will make this a healthy and balanced meal.

INGREDIENTS

For the chicken marinade

For the tzatziki sauce

For the other ingredients

For the dough

  1. 1Combine all the ingredients for the chicken marinade in a bowl and stir together until combined. Add in the cubed chicken and let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. 2While the chicken marinades make the tzatziki sauce. Stir together all of the ingredients and taste for seasoning. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. 4In a large skillet over medium-high heat add enough olive oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Add in the chicken and spread it into a single layer. Let it brown for 3-4 minutes before flipping it over. Continue to cook for another 4-5 minutes or until it's cooked through.
  4. 5To assemble the bowls, fill the bowl with rice and then top it with some of the chicken, cucumbers, red onion, tomatoes, kalamata olives, crumbled feta, and tzatziki sauce.

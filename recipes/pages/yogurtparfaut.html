Yogurt Parfait

  • 4 Servings
  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Inspired by lilluna.com

This recipe includes a ton of delicious fruits. As mentioned before, you can source these ingredients from local food markets and farmers. This is not only climate friendly, but also supports your local community. In the case that you are not near one of these markets, many grocery stores include “ugly foods”: slightly deformed foods that would have otherwise been thrown away.

INGREDIENTS

DIRECTIONS

  1. 1Dice strawberries into smaller pieces.
  2. 2Layer yogurt in 4 cups beginning with 1/2 cup yogurt, a sprinkle of granola, a few spoonfuls of chopped strawberries, as well as a few blackberries and blueberries.
  3. 3Repeat layers one more time and refrigerate until ready to serve.

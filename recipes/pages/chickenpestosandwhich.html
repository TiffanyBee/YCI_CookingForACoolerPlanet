Chicken Pesto Sandwhiches

  • 4 Servings
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes

Chicken produces significantly less carbon emissions compared to your typical lunch meat, making this the Chicken Pesto Sandwich a great quick meal for lunch. If you crave any additional vegetables on your sandwich, this could be a great opportunity to pay your local farmers market a visit.

INGREDIENTS

For the basil pesto

DIRECTIONS

  1. 1Combine basil, garlic, pine nuts and Parmesan in the bowl of a food processor; season with salt and pepper, to taste. With the motor running, add olive oil in a slow stream until emulsified; set aside.
  2. 2In a large bowl, combine chicken, 1/2 cup pesto, Greek yogurt, salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. 3Serve sandwiches on baguette with arugula, tomatoes, mozzarella and chicken pesto mixture.
